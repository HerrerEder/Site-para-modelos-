Sobre Anna Bella

Ask yourself: ‘Do I have a reputation for being mild-tempered and humble? Do I willingly do menial jobs to serve others? Am I kind to others?’ Jesus created a peaceful, inviting environment for his fellow workers, and he enjoyed training them. He encouraged his disciples to ask questions, and he wanted to hear their opinions. Much like plants in a greenhouse, the disciples flourished.
